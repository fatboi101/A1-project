My interest in IT has started from a very young age, being around computers and playing video games my entire life. Being surrounded by technology has caused me to eventually become interested in how these machines work, why they work, and how they can be useful to people around the world in different scenarios. Currently my interest in IT lies in the security aspect of it, how to keep my own information safe and how to also protect companies from hackers and other virtual attacks. I am also interested with the vast number of programs and technologies that are currently being used and want to help further the use of IT services and products in the world.

The event that sparked my interest in pursuing Information Technology is the lack of motivation to continue my previous course of Science at RMIT. This caused me to research other possible courses to transfer into and to revaluate what I wanted to do in the future. The Bachelor of IT at RMIT was the first preference in what I wanted to do after dropping my previous course. My IT experience has only been through a robotics subject, and a game development subject in High School. Most of the things that I have learnt were forgotten though.
